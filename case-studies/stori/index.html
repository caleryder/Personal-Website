Building Trust at Scale: Transforming Stori into a Unified Financial Platform

ROLE VP OF DESIGN, RESEARCH, CONTENT · REMOTE
YEARS 2024–PRESENT

I turned four disconnected business units into one cohesive platform, building the systems and rigor that fueled growth without sacrificing user confidence.

The challenge

Stori had successfully scaled to millions of customers across LATAM, expanding from a credit card provider into a broader financial platform spanning credit cards, loans, deposits, bill payments, remittances, phone top-ups, credit health, and other everyday financial services. However, rapid growth across independent financial products created fragmented experiences that challenged trust, engagement, and cross-product adoption. I led the transformation from disconnected services into a unified financial platform designed to improve customer confidence and unlock sustainable growth.

My role as a leader

I defined and executed a unified vision for the app experience, scaling the systems, processes, and capabilities needed to improve customer trust and business growth. Leading a team of 18 across research, content, and product design in LATAM, I partnered with executives and cross-functional leaders to identify the highest-impact customer opportunities and align teams around a shared roadmap. Beyond product delivery, I evolved how the organization worked, introducing stronger UX measurement, faster learning cycles, quality practices, and AI-enabled workflows to increase design impact and accelerate outcomes.

The outcome

Established the strategic foundation for a unified customer experience, enabling the organization to deliver with greater consistency, speed, and confidence. Built lasting systems, operating models, and AI capabilities that improved how teams worked together, raised the quality bar across products, and positioned the company for its next phase of growth as a platform. Along the way, I developed leaders, introduced AI into the team's practice, and expanded my role into product leadership to maximize business impact.
