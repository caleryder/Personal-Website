Turning Business Insights into Better Decisions

ROLE SR PRODUCT DESIGN MANAGER · REMOTE
YEARS 2022–2024

I brought fragmented teams together around a shared customer strategy, transforming disconnected dashboard efforts into a unified insights experience that helped businesses understand, decide, and act.

The challenge

Meta's Insights Group supported advertisers across multiple products, but each team approached insights differently. Teams built dashboards independently, creating fragmented experiences and inconsistent guidance for businesses trying to understand their performance. The opportunity wasn't to build more detailed dashboards based on available data, it was to create a shared strategy for helping businesses understand what happened, why it happened, and what to do next.

My role as a leader

I led a team of ~15 designers across four product organizations while partnering with Product, Engineering, Research, and Data Science leaders to align strategy for Meta's Business Insights platform. By convening cross-functional leaders around a shared customer vision, I helped replace fragmented roadmaps with a common direction that accelerated execution and improved business outcomes.

Strategic impact
Aligned Cross-Functional Leaders Around a Shared Customer Strategy

With teams building insights independently, I recognized that alignment required more than another planning exercise. I convened cross-functional leaders from Product, Design, Engineering, Research, and Data Science in a multi-day strategy sprint, bringing together customer insights, business priorities, and outside-industry examples to reframe how Meta should deliver value through insights. I then developed a simple framework centered on three customer questions: What happened? Why? What should I do next? — giving teams a shared language that aligned problems to solve, roadmap priorities, and accelerated execution.

Applied the framework to Meta Business Suite, where businesses track performance, helping the team redesign insights around customer next steps instead of metrics. The work connected insights directly to recommended actions, like creating an ad, significantly exceeding business goals.

The outcome

Shifted the organization from building dashboards to designing decision-support experiences, creating a shared foundation for teams to deliver insights that helped businesses understand performance, take action, and grow.
